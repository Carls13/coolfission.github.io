Getting a Permit in GA

Obtaining a permit in Georgia is a lot easier than one may think. In my personal experiences, it has actually taken a lot longer for me to get all of the neccessary documents than actually passing the test.

Requirements=

The first thing that you want to do when applying for a permit, is to know if you are eligible. In Georgia, you must be at least 15 years old and a legal resident of the state.

Next, you want to do make sure you have all of the documents needed

This includes:

  • US Passport
  • School Certificate of Attendance (Can be picked up in an attendance office at school
  • Social Security Card

You can get more information here: https://www.dmv.org/ga-georgia/drivers-permits.php
