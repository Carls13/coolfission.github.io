Conjugating French Verbs

  • Just like the English language, there are also verbs in French. However, the way these verbs are said and wrote are different. In French, verbs need to be conjugated, or changed according to whoever is speaking.
  • This website makes it easy to understand basic conjugations for French.
  • In French, there are two types of verbs, regular verbs and irreguar verbs. Regular verbs have a specific pattern for conjugating. While irregular verbs don't follow any pattern in particular.
  • Each of these verbs can be subdivided into further categories
  • Click to expand on each accordion to learn more.
    • IMPORTANT: This website doesn't show the conjugations in the past of future tense, only in the present for now.

    Regular -er verbs


    Format for regular -er verbs
    Je -e Nous -ons
    Tu -es Vous -ez
    Il/Elle -e Ils/Elles -ent
